Everything We Provide

We offer flexible, compassionate care across a wide range of non-medical services — all designed around you.

Personal Care Assistance

  • Bathing and showering assistance
  • Dressing and grooming support
  • Hair and skin care
  • Toileting and incontinence support
  • Mobility assistance
  • Oral hygiene
  • Meal setup and feeding support

Companionship & Social Engagement

  • Meaningful conversation and active listening
  • Social outings and community involvement
  • Faith service accompaniment
  • Reading, games, and television
  • Hobby encouragement and support
  • Emotional wellness and support

Homemaking & Household Support

  • Laundry and linen changes
  • Vacuuming and dusting
  • Dishwashing and kitchen cleanup
  • Home organization
  • Trash removal
  • Mail and package collection
  • Light pet and plant care

Meal Planning & Preparation

  • Daily meal preparation
  • Light grocery shopping
  • Hydration reminders
  • Post-meal cleanup
  • Special and therapeutic diet accommodation

Errands & Transportation

  • Non-medical doctor and specialist appointments
  • Grocery and pharmacy runs
  • Salon and barber visits
  • Post office and bank errands
  • Prescription pickup

Medication Reminders

  • Verbal medication reminders
  • Pill box organization assistance
We do not dispense or administer medications. Our companions provide reminders only.

Respite Care for Family Caregivers

  • Daily care coverage while family caregivers rest
  • Overnight care options
  • Flexible scheduling for temporary or continuous relief
  • Peace of mind knowing your loved one is in good hands

Specialized Non-Medical Support

  • Dementia and Alzheimer's companionship
  • Hospice partnership and end-of-life support
  • Post-hospital discharge assistance
  • Behavioral understanding and gentle redirection

Flexible Scheduling — No Long-Term Contracts

Daily, overnight, and 24/7 care options are available. We build care plans tailored to your needs and schedule, with no long-term commitment required. Start whenever you're ready.
