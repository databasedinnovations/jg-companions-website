Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Effective Date: 2025  |  Business: Jollygood Companion Services

1. Information We Collect

We gather only voluntary data submitted through our website's consultation forms, including your name, email address, phone number, and message content. Additionally, our website host may automatically capture non-personal analytics data per its own privacy practices.

2. How We Use Your Information

Personal data you share with us is used solely to respond to your inquiries or consultation requests and to communicate with you about our services.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored through our website platform's infrastructure, which uses industry-standard security protections. We retain only the data submitted through website forms and do not maintain personal data beyond what is necessary to respond to your inquiry.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website platform may deploy cookies and tracking technologies for site operations and traffic analysis. No additional tracking tools beyond the platform's built-in features are utilized. You may adjust cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at angel@jollygoodcompanions.com or call (913) 608-7373.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (such as partner websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of this website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
