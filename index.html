What We Offer

Our Care Services

Discover the benefits of our home companions — tailored support that adapts to your schedule and needs.

Personal Care Assistance

Support with bathing, dressing, grooming, hair & skin care, toileting, mobility assistance, oral hygiene, and meal setup.

Companionship & Social Engagement

Conversation, social outings, games, entertainment, hobby encouragement, and emotional wellness support.

Homemaking & Household Support

Laundry, vacuuming, dusting, dishwashing, organization, trash removal, mail collection, and light pet & plant care.

Meal Planning & Preparation

Daily meal prep, grocery shopping, hydration reminders, post-meal cleanup, and special diet accommodation.

Errands & Transportation

Non-medical rides to doctor appointments, grocery runs, pharmacy visits, salons, and post office errands.

Medication Reminders

Verbal reminders and pill box organization assistance. We do not dispense or administer medications.

Respite Care for Family Caregivers

Daily or overnight care with flexible scheduling, providing temporary or continuous relief for family caregivers.

Specialized Non-Medical Support

Dementia & Alzheimer's companionship, hospice partnership end-of-life support, and post-hospital discharge assistance.

Flexible Scheduling

Daily, overnight, and 24/7 care options available with flexible care plans — and no long-term contracts required.
